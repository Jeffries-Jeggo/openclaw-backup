## Key UDL Concepts for Lesson Planning

### Three Principles:
1. **Engagement** - Multiple means of engagement (why)
2. **Representation** - Multiple means of representation (what)
3. **Action & Expression** - Multiple means of action & expression (how)

### Four Components of UDL Curriculum:
- Goals (firm goals, flexible means)
- Methods (instructional approaches)
- Materials (resources and media)
- Assessments (multiple ways to demonstrate learning)

### Ready to Use:
When lesson planning, search these files for:
- Ways to provide student choice
- Strategies for engagement
- Methods for representation
- Options for expression/assessment
- How to create inclusive learning environments

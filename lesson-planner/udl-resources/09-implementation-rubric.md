# UDL Implementation Rubric

UDL isn't a framework that you can implement overnight. It takes years – not weeks or months – to reach expert level.

## Self-Evaluation Questions

- What are your next steps in terms of implementation?
- Based on your current level of practice, what is one goal you have for today?

Making commitments after self-evaluation is a wonderful way to get started. Remember, UDL implementation is a learning process that takes engagement, motivation, grit, resourcefulness, and creativity.

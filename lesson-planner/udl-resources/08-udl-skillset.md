# The Skillset of a UDL Practitioner

What capacities does an educator need to have to remove barriers to learning when designing Tier 1 instruction that is inclusive and equitable?

## How to Use This Skillset Tool

1. Share in faculty meetings or PD sessions as a self-assessment
2. Use to vet current professional development offerings
3. Use to inspire action-oriented feedback within observations

## Key Areas

The tool looks at the UDL 3.0 Guidelines, which identify predictable barriers that may prevent all learners from reaching high expectations. Then it examines evidence-based research on best practices for eliminating those barriers.

Note: This tool is focused on first, best instruction. To meet the needs of all learners, it's critical that the system supplements targeted intervention and enrichment.

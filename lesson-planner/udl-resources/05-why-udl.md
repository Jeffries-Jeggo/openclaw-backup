# Why Implement UDL?

"We are preparing students for their future. We are not preparing them for our past."

The way that we have been teaching students for decades will no longer meet their needs because we are preparing them for jobs that don't even exist yet and technology that is replaced daily.

## The Shift Needed

In this new world, we have to teach our students how to be thinkers, love challenge, and commit to innovation. We cannot do this when we, as educators, are in charge of their learning.

## The Problem with Traditional Teaching

Traditional teaching prepares students to be compliant and to do what the teacher tells them to do. This is not intentional, but it's the hidden curriculum.

## The UDL Approach

When educators step out of the spotlight, they can:
- Craft and personalize their message to all students
- Allow students to collaborate and connect with the world
- Take learning into the community
- Give students feedback and challenge them
- Help students find their way in a world that doesn't exist yet

UDL is a full circle. It's not about compliance, it's about engagement — and engagement means everyone has firm goals and flexible means.
